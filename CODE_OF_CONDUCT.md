# Code Of Conduct

Emberlane follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant: be respectful, welcoming, and constructive.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Using kind, direct, and inclusive language.
- Assuming good intent while still being clear about risks.
- Giving actionable feedback focused on code, docs, tests, and user outcomes.
- Respecting different experience levels with Rust, AWS, Terraform, and LLM infrastructure.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publicly sharing private information or secrets.
- Dismissing safety, cost, or security concerns.
- Repeatedly pushing unrelated product scope after maintainers have clarified direction.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the same channels listed in `SECURITY.md` until a project-specific contact is published.
